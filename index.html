ART IN/OF PROTEST

CREATIVE PROTESTS

An interactive map compiling examples and case studies of protest where the protestors used art and creativity to get their point across. Art has the capacity to break barriers and unite people for the cause non violently.

“When it gets down to having to use violence, then you are playing the system’s game. The establishment will irritate you – pull your beard, flick your face – to make you fight. Because once they’ve got you violent, then they know how to handle you.”

JOHN LENNON

Click on the red icons on the map to know more about these particular events.

The map was compiled by the students of UPID NOIDA, 3rd Semester under the Architectural Studies in Design course. The aim of the project was to understand how design and creativity can be used as an alternative to conventional means of getting oue points across.
